Our Story

Dee's Local Bar & Pastries is a proudly Ghanaian business located in Odupong Ofaakor, Kasoa. We specialize in traditional and modern treats, offering a delicious range of baked goods, refreshing local drinks, and full event catering services.


What We Do

  • Bakery:Wedding Cakes,Aniversary Cakes, Birthday Cakes, Sweet Buns, Meat Pies, Chips, Spring Rolls.
  • Drinks: Sobolo, asaana, Lamujin, Pineapple Juice, Tigernut Pudding, Mango Juice, Watermelon Jiuce, Caropine, Fruit Mix etc.
  • Events: Setup and Serving for Weddings, Engagements, Naming Ceremonies, and other occasions.

Our Mission

We believe in delivering joy through food. Whether it's a birthday celebration or a traditional ceremony, Dee Local brings flavor, warmth, and professionalism to every event.


Values

  • Quality ingredients
  • Friendly service
  • Clean and comfortable environment
  • Community connection
